Why I started my crafts

During the summer time, I would travel up to Canada to stay with my grandma and I got to a certain point where alot of things would bore me... Until one day my grandma taught me how to knit. She told me a very basic knitting style for a hat and even though I didn't actually follow the pattern I turned it into a scarf.
